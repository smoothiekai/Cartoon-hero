Jake the Dog

About Jake

Jake "Jacob" the Dog is a magical dog with the ability to shape-shift. He and his best friend Finn - who is also his adoptive brother - live in the Tree House with a computer called BMO. Jake is in relationship with Lady Rainicorn and they have five magical pups together.

Friends

  • Finn
  • BMO
  • Lady Rainicorn
  • Princess Bubblegum
  • Marceline the Vampire Queen

Hobbies and interests

Jake is talented in singing, dancing and playing the violin. He is passionate about a board game called Card Wars and, playing the violin, but most importantly about food and cooking. Jake can also speak Korean since that is how he communicates with Lady Rainicorn and her parents.
